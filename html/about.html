What is meant by Karmyog?

Karma yoga is one of the four classical schools of yoga alongside Jnana (knowledge or self-study), Bhakti (devotion) and Raja (meditation), each offering a path to moksha (spiritual liberation) and self-realization. Derived from the Sanskrit term for "action", karma is understood by both Hindu and Buddhist traditions to be the sum of a personâ€™s deeds in past, present and future states of existence. In yoga, karma is known as the path of action, or selfless service towards others. Karma yoga is considered to be one of the most practical and effective means of spiritual development as explained by hindu god Shri Krishna in Shreemad Bhagwat Geeta.

About the Website

The website Karmyog is a beautifully crafted todo app designed to help you be more productive. It lets you add and manage your todo items and your data is synced across all your devices.
